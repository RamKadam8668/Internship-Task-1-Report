**Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce customer transaction data to understand purchasing behavior, segment customers, and identify retention and churn patterns. The analysis was completed as part of an internship task at Alfido Tech, with the goal of generating actionable business insights using data analytics techniques.

 **Objectives**
- Analyze customer purchase behavior and spending patterns.
- Perform data cleaning and feature engineering.
- Segment customers using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis.
- Study customer retention and churn trends.
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer engagement.

**Dataset**

Source: Kaggle (Customer Behavior Analysis Dataset)
- Data includes customer transactions, product categories, payment methods, purchase dates, and monetary values.

**Methodology**
1) **Data Preprocessing**
- Handling missing values and duplicates.
- Converting data types and creating derived features.

2) **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
- Category-wise sales analysis.
- Monthly customer and revenue trends.
- Distribution of purchase amounts and churn.

3) **Customer Segmentation**
- RFM metric calculation.
- RFM scoring and customer segment profiling.

4) **Retention & Churn Analysis**
-Month-over-month repeat customer analysis.
-Churn identification based on inactivity.

**Key Insights**
- High-value customers contribute a major share of revenue.
- Certain product categories dominate sales performance.
- A significant portion of customers show churn risk.
- Retention-focused strategies are critical for sustained growth.

**Business Recommendations**
- Target high-value and loyal customers with personalized offers.
- Re-engage at-risk customers through promotions and reminders.
- Improve retention strategies to reduce customer churn.

- Jupyter Notebook.
